The Memorial

Mr. Sellappah Tharmalingam born on the 26th of September 1939 is a native of the picturesque town of Velanai, which is just off the coast of Jaffna. With such close access to the sea it is no surprise that Mr. Tharmalingam’s pass time was swimming and playing in the sand. He was the third child in a family of four an elder sister and two brothers grew up in very close bond among them. The eldest his sister, Rajeshwari Sivaloganathan whom he attaches a very close bonding supported him very much financially and morally through his degree at the Colombo University and mentor to him in life. His eldest brother Rajalingam a retired principal and his youngest brother Shanmugalingam a retired teacher pre-deceased in 1997 and 1995 respectively. His wife Kamaladevi ... retired teacher passed away in 2016. After obtaining his Bachelor of Science Degree from the Peradeniya University he continued on the teaching staff. Following on, in 1960 he began the Brighton Institute Colombo to provide additional tuition to students. The institute provided inspiration, motivations and the students were encouraged to achieve their best in studies. They were helped to formulate targets and were motivated to work towards them. The students were self-disciplined individuals and in turn encouraged others to achieve their standard. Many students entered the medical and engineering faculties of the University. The Brighton Institute Colombo went on to be the institute where the City and Guilds of London examinations were held. That in itself speaks volumes about standards of education and integrity maintained by Mr Sellappah Tharmalingam. In 1981 Mr Sellappah Tharmalingam moved to Nigeria as a Physics teacher and in 1986 he moved to UK where he was a physics teacher until 1990 and continued to be domiciled in the UK until his demise on 11th January 2022 at the age of 84. Sellappah Tharmalingam’s only daughter Dr. Satha Bhanu Janagan a General Practitioner in the United Kingdom. Being the only child in the family she all the attention and inspiration from her parents to achieve her best potential at a younger age. She achieved 4 A’s in science in spite of being three years younger than the normal age and entered Colombo medical faculty on merit basis. This site is developed by her and she works towards dedicating it to her father who was her great coach and motivator at every step of her life. She would like the resources to help every student to excel in their studies and achieve the best potential, fulfilling her parent’s dreams of providing every child with the opportunity of a quality education and helping achieve their ambitions in life.
